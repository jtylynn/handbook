# Product Manifesto

**At SOE, our core product is our people and the value we provide is clarity.**

Prioritizing clarity means that SOE will always be accessible and unscary for our customers. It empowers them to know we are taking care of their projects and are delivering on our promises. Clarity requires deep empathy and constant refinement. It does not always make SOE faster or the cheapest option, but instead, has a positive impact on the way our customers *feel*.
